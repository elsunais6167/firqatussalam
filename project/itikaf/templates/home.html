KA'IDOJIN SHIGA ITTIKAF

1. Wajibi ne ga masuyin ittikafi su chika form tare da hoto da kuma katin dan-kasa ko katin zabe.

2. Wajibi ne ga masuyin ittikafi suyi guzurin abubuwan bukatarsu da kuma mai kawo masu abinchi (Ga Mata, Ba'a yarda namiji ya rinka kawo maki abinchi saida karamin yaro ).

3. Babu chin abinchi a cikin masallaci sai bisa leda

4. Ba'a fitowa dada cikin masallaci saida wata lalura ko bukata ta musamman

5. Ba'a firar duniya ga masu ittikafi ko zaman fira ga masu kawo abinchi.

6. Babu shan taba da sauransu a harabar masallaci ko kewaye kuma kada mata su shagala da kitso da kunshi a cikin masallaci.

7. Babu dora kaya a wurin da ake aje takalma, ko kuma yin amfani candle a masallaci.

8. Ba'a yarda a rika amfani da waya (Handset) ba a lokacin ittikafi. Idan kuma yazama lalura, to a sanyata a matakin (Silent) ko (Vibration)

9. Za'ayi biyayya ga umarnin Agaji dana Hisbah da sauran Jami'an tsaro
